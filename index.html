Lincko

Cross-platform, multi-language project management and collaboration tool to help you organize your team's tasks and ideas on the cloud. Tech

Redapp.co

Distribution service for foreign apps entering the Chinese Android app market. As the CTO, I am in charge of using technology to make the business operations as seamless as possible. Webforms were created for clients to submit their app's data such as name, description and screenshots. There is a dashboard where clients can login to keep track of their app's distribution progress in China.

Tech

The static page and blogs are hosted on Firebase Hosting. Client dashboard frontend is developed using Vue.js on the front, and the backend is developed on NodeJS and Firebase Firestore database. Gmail SMTP api is used to automatically generate emails to clients as well as Redapp employees with relevant information.
